About Us

In August 2023, a remarkable team emerged in Baku, composed of high school students with a shared devotion to ecological preservation. Their mission was clear: to be the guardians of the city's environment and its animal inhabitants. These young advocates, driven by an innate love for nature, dedicated themselves to rescuing and sheltering distressed animals, extending their care from injured birds to abandoned pets.

Beyond their immediate rescue efforts, we undertook an educational role, organizing workshops and campaigns to enlighten the community about responsible environmental practices. The team nurtured the ecosystem and a sense of unity among the locals through tree-planting initiatives and collaborative clean-up endeavors. With passion and resourcefulness, these students proved that age was no obstacle to becoming stewards of the environment, leaving an indelible mark on Baku's landscape and inspiring a new generation of eco-conscious citizens.
